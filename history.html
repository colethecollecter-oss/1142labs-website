PHASE I: FOUNDATION
DECEMBER 22, 2019

THE FOUNDING

1142 LABS is born from a singular vision: to bridge the gap between neuroscience, pharmacology, and consciousness research. Founded by Cole EverDark, the lab emerges not from traditional academia, but from lived experience—from the intersection of personal struggle and scientific curiosity.

  • Initial focus on methylphenidate pharmacology
  • Core question: How do stimulants actually work?
  • Harm reduction framework development begins
PHASE I: FOUNDATION
EARLY 2020

FIRST BREAKTHROUGHS

The first comprehensive research papers emerge, exploring methylphenidate mechanisms, dopamine dysregulation, and the neurochemical basis of ADHD. The relationship between dopamine transporter blockade and cognitive enhancement proves more nuanced than mainstream understanding suggests.

  • Research Papers #1-4 published
  • Dopamine and executive function explored
  • Stimulant withdrawal protocols developed
  • First tapering calculator created
PHASE II: EXPANSION
LATE 2020 - EARLY 2021

NEURODIVERSITY FRAMEWORK

A philosophical shift occurs: moving from "disorder" language to neurodiversity-affirming perspectives. ADHD and Autism are explored not as deficits, but as different ways of processing the world. The community begins to grow.

  • Research Papers #5-6: Beyond the deficit model
  • ADHD and Autism overlap explored
  • Neurodivergent community connections formed
  • Compassionate science framework established
PHASE II: EXPANSION
MID 2021

CONSCIOUSNESS RESEARCH BEGINS

Expanding beyond ADHD into broader consciousness studies. Psychedelics, neuroplasticity, and the neuroscience of transcendence become new territories. Phenomenology meets rigorous neuroscience.

  • Research Papers #7-8: Psychedelics & DMT
  • Neuroplasticity mechanisms explored
  • Integration of subjective experience with science
  • New research methodologies developed
PHASE II: EXPANSION
LATE 2021

MENTAL HEALTH DEEP DIVE

Comprehensive exploration of depression, PTSD, and anxiety from a neuroplasticity perspective. Combining rigorous neuroscience with empathetic understanding. The "Smiling Depression" paper resonates deeply with readers.

THE PHILOSOPHY OF 1142

1. Science Without Stigma

Neuroscience research that treats mental health conditions with respect and compassion, never pathologizing human experience.

2. Harm Reduction First

Practical, non-judgmental information that helps people make safer choices about their neurobiology and medication use.

3. Neurodiversity Affirmation

Celebrating different ways of thinking and being, moving beyond deficit-based models of ADHD, autism, and other neurodivergences.

4. Open Access Knowledge

Making complex neuroscience accessible to everyone, not just academics. Free resources, clear explanations, no paywalls.

5. Lived Experience Matters

Integrating personal experience with scientific research, acknowledging that those who live with conditions have valuable insights.

6. Aesthetic Intentionality

The visual language of 1142—cyberpunk, neon, futuristic—reflects the belief that science can be both rigorous and beautiful.

LEGACY AND IMPACT

1142 LABS represents more than research papers and calculators. It represents a commitment to democratizing neuroscience knowledge, reducing harm in medication use, celebrating neurodiversity, bridging science and art, supporting those who struggle, and advancing human understanding.

From a single founding moment on December 22, 2019, to a comprehensive digital laboratory in 2026, 1142 has grown into a beacon for those seeking to understand their minds, their medications, and their place in the universe.

THE WORK CONTINUES. THE FRONTIER EXPANDS. THE FUTURE IS 1142.
