Disorders & Disabilities

Understanding Mental Health, Neurodevelopment & Trauma

Our Approach

At 1142 Labs, we approach mental health conditions not as deficits to be cured, but as different neurological configurations that come with unique challenges and strengths. We embrace the neurodiversity paradigm while providing evidence-based information about diagnosis, treatment, and support.

Our research explores innovative approaches to mental health—from psychedelic-assisted therapy to neurostimulant optimization—always grounded in rigorous science and compassionate care.

Wired Differently

Mental health conditions and neurodevelopmental differences are not moral failings, character flaws, or signs of weakness. They are variations in human neurology—different ways of processing, experiencing, and interacting with the world.

At 1142 Labs, we celebrate neurodiversity while acknowledging the real challenges these conditions can present. Our goal is to provide information, support, and research that empowers individuals to thrive on their own terms.

You are not broken. You are wired differently. And that's okay.

Crisis Resources

If you're in crisis or experiencing suicidal thoughts:

  • ✦ 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline: Call or text 988 (24/7)
  • ✦ Crisis Text Line: Text HOME to 741741
  • ✦ Veterans Crisis Line: Call 988 and press 1
  • ✦ Emergency: Call 911 or go to nearest emergency room
  • ✦ SAMHSA National Helpline: 1-800-662-4357 (treatment referral)

You are not alone. Help is available 24/7.
