Autism Spectrum Disorder

Understanding Neurodiversity & The Spectrum of Human Experience

What is Autism?

Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) is a neurodevelopmental condition characterized by differences in social communication, sensory processing, and patterns of behavior and interests. The term "spectrum" reflects the wide variation in challenges and strengths possessed by each individual with autism.

At 1142 Labs, we embrace the neurodiversity paradigm—recognizing autism not as a disorder to be cured, but as a different neurological configuration that brings unique perspectives, abilities, and ways of experiencing the world.

The Evolution of Autism Understanding

"From Low-Functioning to High-Functioning: The Evolution of Autism"

Historical classifications of autism have evolved significantly. What was once labeled "low-functioning" or "high-functioning" autism is now understood as a complex interplay of support needs, environmental factors, and individual strengths that vary across contexts and time.

Our research explores how neurostimulants and targeted interventions can support autistic individuals in accessing their full potential, not by "fixing" autism, but by optimizing their unique neurological architecture.

Core Characteristics

Social Communication

  • ✦ Different patterns of eye contact
  • ✦ Unique communication styles
  • ✦ Difficulty with social reciprocity
  • ✦ Preference for direct communication
  • ✦ Challenges with nonverbal cues

Sensory Processing

  • ✦ Hyper- or hypo-sensitivity to stimuli
  • ✦ Unique sensory preferences
  • ✦ Sensory seeking or avoiding behaviors
  • ✦ Enhanced perception in specific domains
  • ✦ Sensory overload experiences

Patterns & Interests

  • ✦ Intense focused interests
  • ✦ Preference for routine and predictability
  • ✦ Repetitive behaviors (stimming)
  • ✦ Systematic thinking patterns
  • ✦ Detail-oriented perception

Autistic Strengths & Abilities

Pattern Recognition

Exceptional ability to identify patterns, systems, and logical structures that others may miss.

Attention to Detail

Enhanced perception of fine details and ability to maintain focus on complex information.

Specialized Knowledge

Deep expertise in areas of interest, often reaching professional-level understanding.

Authentic Expression

Direct, honest communication and genuine emotional expression without social masking.

Innovative Thinking

Unique perspectives that lead to creative problem-solving and novel approaches.

Loyalty & Dedication

Strong commitment to people, projects, and principles that matter.

Support & Accommodations

Environmental

Sensory-friendly spaces, predictable routines, clear communication, and reduced sensory overload.

Educational

Individualized learning approaches, visual supports, special interests integration, and executive function aids.

Social

Clear expectations, social scripts, neurodivergent-affirming spaces, and authentic connection opportunities.

Therapeutic

Occupational therapy, speech therapy, cognitive-behavioral approaches, and strengths-based interventions.

Wired Differently

At 1142 Labs, we celebrate neurodiversity.

Autistic individuals are not broken versions of neurotypical people—they are differently wired, with unique strengths, perspectives, and ways of processing the world. Our research aims to support autistic individuals in thriving on their own terms, not in conforming to neurotypical standards.

We believe that neurodiversity is a strength, not a deficit. The future belongs to those who think differently.
