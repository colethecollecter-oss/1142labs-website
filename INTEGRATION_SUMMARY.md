## Design & Aesthetic System

### The Ritual Design System

Based on the 1142 Unified Manuscript, the site implements a **six-phase ritual journey**:

1. **Origin** - Sepia tones, memory decay aesthetic
2. **Fall** - Red glitch effects, fractured typography
3. **Resurrection** - Neon cyan and pink, pulsing glyphs
4. **Transformation** - Black-and-white inversion
5. **Legend** - Cosmic synthwave palettes
6. **Closing** - Ethereal golden glow, holographic shimmer
