Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder: Understanding the Spectrum

What is ADHD?

Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder (ADHD) is a neurodevelopmental condition characterized by persistent patterns of inattention, hyperactivity, and impulsivity that interfere with functioning or development.

ADHD affects millions of individuals worldwide and manifests differently across the lifespan. At 1142 Labs, we approach ADHD not as a deficit, but as a different neurological configuration with unique strengths and challenges.

ADHD Presentations

Predominantly Inattentive

  • ✦ Difficulty sustaining attention
  • ✦ Easily distracted by external stimuli
  • ✦ Forgetfulness in daily activities
  • ✦ Difficulty organizing tasks
  • ✦ Avoidance of sustained mental effort

Predominantly Hyperactive-Impulsive

  • ✦ Fidgeting and restlessness
  • ✦ Difficulty remaining seated
  • ✦ Excessive talking
  • ✦ Interrupting or intruding on others
  • ✦ Difficulty waiting turn

Combined Presentation

  • ✦ Symptoms from both categories
  • ✦ Most common presentation
  • ✦ Variable symptom intensity
  • ✦ Context-dependent manifestation
  • ✦ Requires comprehensive management

ADHD Strengths & Advantages

Hyperfocus

Intense concentration on tasks of interest, leading to exceptional productivity and deep work capabilities.

Creativity

Divergent thinking patterns that generate novel solutions and innovative approaches to problems.

High Energy

Natural enthusiasm and drive that, when channeled effectively, produces remarkable achievements.

Adaptability

Rapid cognitive shifting and flexibility in dynamic environments requiring quick decision-making.

Management Strategies

Pharmacological

Stimulant medications (methylphenidate, amphetamines) and non-stimulant options tailored to individual neurochemistry.

Behavioral

Cognitive-behavioral therapy, organizational skills training, and environmental modifications.

Lifestyle

Exercise, sleep optimization, nutrition, and mindfulness practices that support executive function.

Technology

Digital tools, apps, and systems designed to augment attention and organizational capabilities.
